Print of The Month

Jamie Perry

Contemporary American Realist Painter

Prints

Archival pigment prints are produced on heavy-weight cotton poly-blend canvas (395 grams per square meter). Each limited edition print of 50 is signed by Jamie Perry. Prints as priced are unframed, and are rolled and shipped in a postal tube.

Shipping & Return Policy

Thank you for your interest in art by Jamie Perry.

Shipping

Artwork will be shipped by UPS or US Mail. Items shipped within the United States are billed at a flat of $25; international orders are sent via Express Shipping and billed at a flat rate of $60. Orders should arrive within 14 business days. Exceptions to delivery timelines or special orders will be discussed with the collector.

Returns and Exchanges

If for any reason, you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item or exchange it for the purchase price. Shipping fees will not be refunded. To return an item please notify us by email at perry.paints@yahoo.com within 7 business days following receipt of the item. Returned items must be in an unused, sellable condition.

Artist's Statement

Most of my paintings are comprised of two main elements...light and space. A solitary subject illuminated on a vast plane of darkness invites the viewer to feel a sense of isolation, but at the same time, warmth and safety in the light, with freedom to move about in the seemingly boundless space.

An open road leading to a vanishing point symbolizes the future, and the journey that we all take to get there. My aim is to create work that evokes sentimental feelings in the viewer, and transcends them to a place where life is simple and unobstructed.
